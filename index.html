PPP Dimension Selector Self-check

Identify which PPP dimensions may be most useful to focus on

Use this quick self-check to reflect on how strongly each of the PPP dimensions currently shows up in your teaching. It should take around 5–7 minutes. Rate each statement on a scale from 1–5:

  • 1 = Strongly disagree
  • 2 = Disagree
  • 3 = Neutral / unsure
  • 4 = Agree
  • 5 = Strongly agree

Some items are phrased positively and some negatively. The tool automatically adjusts the scoring so that lower overall scores still indicate more room for growth. This is for your own reflection only – responses are not stored or submitted.
